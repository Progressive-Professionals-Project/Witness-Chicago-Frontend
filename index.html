About This Platform

Federal agents and National Guard units are operating unlawfully in Chicago without proper identification. If you witness or experience civil rights violations you can report them here. Reports will be investigated by civil rights attorneys who may reach out to you if you provide contact information.

When to Submit a Report:

  • Unlawful detention or arrest without proper identification
  • Excessive force by unidentified federal agents
  • Searches without warrants or probable cause
  • Denial of legal representation or medical care
  • Intimidation, harassment, or other civil rights violations

Privacy:

  • Your data is encrypted before being sent
  • Providing your contact information is optional
  • Reports are made available to civil rights attorneys
  • Consider using private browsing mode
